## Booking

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I make a reservation?">
    To make a reservation, use the booking form on the Pedal website. You will need to provide the following information:

    - **Car type** — choose from Compacto, Sedán, SUV, or Camioneta
    - **Date of use** — the date you need the vehicle
    - **Pickup time** — the time you will collect the vehicle
    - **Pickup location** — choose from Miraflores, San Isidro, or San Miguel
    - **Return time** — the time you will bring the vehicle back
    - **Return location** — choose from Miraflores, San Isidro, or San Miguel

    Once you submit the form, the Pedal team will process your request and confirm availability.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What car types are available?">
    Pedal offers four car categories to suit different needs:

    - **Compacto** — compact cars ideal for city driving and tight parking
    - **Sedán** — standard four-door sedans suitable for everyday use and longer trips
    - **SUV** — sport utility vehicles with more space and higher ground clearance
    - **Camioneta** — pickup trucks for heavier loads or off-road use

    You can filter by car type when filling out the booking form.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I return the vehicle to a different location than where I picked it up?">
    Yes. The pickup location and return location are selected independently in the booking form. You can pick up your vehicle at one of the three available locations — Miraflores, San Isidro, or San Miguel — and return it to any of those same locations, including a different one from where you started.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I subscribe to receive promotions?">
    At the bottom of the Pedal website there is a newsletter subscription section. Enter your email address to sign up and be the first to know about car rental promotions. By submitting the form, you accept Pedal's privacy policy and terms of service.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Vehicles

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What car brands does Pedal offer?">
    Pedal's fleet includes vehicles from the following manufacturers:

    - **Honda**
    - **Chevrolet**
    - **Volvo**
    - **Mitsubishi**
    - **Volkswagen**

    Each brand offers multiple models across different categories. See the [Vehicle Fleet](/fleet/overview) section for the full list of available vehicles.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is the price range for rentals?">
    Rental prices at Pedal start at **S/. 600 per day** for the Mitsubishi Mirage and go up to **S/. 1,500 per day** for the Volvo XC90. The full fleet of 15 vehicles spans this range.

    Prices shown as "Desde" (starting from) indicate the base daily rate for that model. Final pricing may vary depending on rental duration and availability.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What fuel types are available?">
    Pedal's fleet includes both petrol and diesel vehicles. The fuel type for each model is listed on the individual vehicle page in the fleet catalog. Make sure to check the fuel type before booking so you know what to expect.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Are manual transmission vehicles available?">
    Yes. Several models in the Pedal fleet are available with manual transmission. The transmission type is listed on each vehicle's detail page. If you have a preference for manual or automatic, check the fleet catalog before selecting your car type in the booking form.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Locations

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Where can I pick up my vehicle?">
    Pedal has three pickup locations, all in Lima:

    - **Miraflores**
    - **San Isidro**
    - **San Miguel**

    These locations are selected when filling out the booking form. Pedal chose these locations for their accessibility and convenience.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What regions does Pedal operate in?">
    Pedal currently provides car rental services across five departments in Peru:

    - **Lima**
    - **Ica**
    - **Arequipa**
    - **Ancash**
    - **Piura**

    Vehicle pickups and returns are handled through the Lima-based locations (Miraflores, San Isidro, San Miguel).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
