## Social media

You can also reach Pedal through social media. Our accounts are monitored regularly and are a convenient way to ask questions or stay up to date with news and promotions.

- **Facebook** — updates, promotions, and customer service
- **WhatsApp** — quick messaging for support and inquiries
- **Instagram** — fleet highlights and company news
- **Twitter/X** — announcements and real-time updates

## Stay informed with our newsletter

Subscribe to the Pedal newsletter to be the first to know about car rental promotions and special offers. The subscription form is located at the bottom of the Pedal website — enter your email address to sign up.

By subscribing, you accept Pedal's privacy policy and terms of service. You can unsubscribe at any time.

## What our support team can help with

The Pedal support team is equipped to assist you with a wide range of questions and issues:

- **Booking questions and modifications** — help with filling out the reservation form, changing pickup or return details, or adjusting your booking
- **Vehicle selection guidance** — advice on choosing the right car type, brand, or model for your needs and budget
- **Location and pickup information** — directions to pickup points in Miraflores, San Isidro, and San Miguel, and answers about our coverage across Lima, Ica, Arequipa, Ancash, and Piura
- **Technical issues with the website** — reporting and resolving problems with the booking form, newsletter signup, or any other part of the Pedal website
