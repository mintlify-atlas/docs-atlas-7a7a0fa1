## Booking and reservations

All reservations must be submitted through the official Pedal booking form on the website. The form requires the following information:

- Car type (Compacto, Sedán, SUV, or Camioneta)
- Date of use
- Pickup time and pickup location
- Return time and return location

Submitting a booking form initiates a reservation request. Vehicle availability is subject to scheduling and is not guaranteed until confirmed by the Pedal team.

Pickup must occur at the selected location and at the scheduled time. Late arrivals may result in the reservation being cancelled or rescheduled, subject to availability.

## Vehicle use

Vehicles must be returned to the agreed return location and at the agreed return time as specified in your booking. Returning a vehicle to an unspecified location or significantly outside the agreed timeframe may incur additional charges.

Each vehicle in the Pedal fleet has a listed fuel type — either petrol or diesel. You are responsible for returning the vehicle with the same fuel level as at the time of pickup, using the correct fuel type as listed for your vehicle.

Vehicles must be used in a lawful manner and in accordance with road traffic regulations in Peru. Any damage, traffic violations, or incidents during the rental period are the responsibility of the renter.

## Locations and coverage

Pedal currently operates across five departments in Peru:

- Lima
- Ica
- Arequipa
- Ancash
- Piura

Vehicle pickups and returns are handled through three designated points, all located in Lima:

- **Miraflores**
- **San Isidro**
- **San Miguel**

Pickup and return outside these designated locations is not currently supported. Pedal's coverage area may expand in the future.

## Pricing

All rental prices are quoted in Peruvian Soles (S/.) and represent per-day rates. Prices range from **S/. 600 per day** for entry-level models such as the Mitsubishi Mirage, up to **S/. 1,500 per day** for premium vehicles such as the Volvo XC90.

Where prices are listed with the label "Desde" (starting from), this indicates the base daily rate for that vehicle. Final pricing may vary based on rental duration, seasonal demand, or promotional offers.

Prices displayed on the website are subject to change. The confirmed rate will be communicated at the time of booking confirmation.
