This guide walks you through everything you need to rent a car on Pedal for the first time. By the end, you will have submitted a booking and know exactly where to go to collect your vehicle.

## Before you begin

Pedal is available to anyone in Peru. No account registration is required to browse the fleet or submit a booking request. You only need access to the Pedal website and the following information ready:

- Your intended travel date
- Pickup and return times
- Preferred pickup and return locations

<Info>
  Pedal currently operates pickup and return points at **Miraflores**, **San Isidro**, and **San Miguel** in the Lima metropolitan area. Service is available across Lima, Ica, Arequipa, Ancash, and Piura.
</Info>

## How to make your first booking

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Pedal website">
    Open the Pedal platform in your browser. The homepage displays the full vehicle catalog along with the booking form. You do not need to log in or create an account.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Browse the vehicle catalog">
    Scroll through the catalog to explore available vehicles. Cars are grouped by brand — Honda, Chevrolet, Volvo, Mitsubishi, and Volkswagen. Each listing shows:

    - **Seating capacity** — how many passengers the car accommodates
    - **Transmission** — Automatic or Manual
    - **Fuel efficiency** — measured in km/L
    - **Daily rate** — price in Peruvian soles (S/.)
    - **Fuel type** — Petrol or Diesel

    Use this information to narrow down the right vehicle before filling in the booking form. See [Choosing the right vehicle](#choosing-the-right-vehicle) below for a full comparison.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select your car type">
    In the booking form, open the **Car type** dropdown and select one of the four categories:

    - **Compacto** — Small, fuel-efficient cars ideal for city driving
    - **Sedán** — Mid-size cars with a comfortable ride for longer trips
    - **SUV** — Versatile vehicles with higher ground clearance and more cargo space
    - **Camioneta** — Large trucks or pickups suited for heavy loads or rough terrain

    <Tip>
      If you are unsure which category a specific model falls under, refer to the [Choosing the right vehicle](#choosing-the-right-vehicle) section for a detailed comparison table.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set your date and pickup time">
    Click the **Fecha de uso** (Date of use) field and select your rental date from the calendar. Then set your **Hora de recoger** (Pickup time) using the time input. Choose a time that gives you enough buffer to arrive at the location comfortably.

    <Note>
      The date field controls the day you pick up the car. If your trip spans multiple days, your return time on the same or a later day determines the total rental duration.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose your pickup location">
    Open the **Lugar de recoger** (Pickup location) dropdown and select one of the three available points:

    - **Miraflores**
    - **San Isidro**
    - **San Miguel**

    See [Pickup locations](#pickup-locations) below for details on each site.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set your return time and location">
    Enter your **Hora de devolver** (Return time) and then select a **Lugar de devolver** (Return location). You can return the vehicle to a different location from where you picked it up — all three points accept returns.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Submit your booking">
    Review all the details you have entered, then click the **Submit** button. The Pedal team will receive your request and confirm availability. You will be contacted at the details you provided to finalise the reservation.

    <Check>
      Once submitted, check your inbox or phone for a confirmation from the Pedal team. They will confirm your booking and provide any additional instructions for collection.
    </Check>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Pickup locations

All Pedal pickup and return points are located in Lima. You can collect and return your vehicle at any of the three locations — they do not need to match.

<Info>
  All three locations serve both pickups and returns. You can pick up in Miraflores and return in San Miguel, for example, with no additional steps required in the booking form.
</Info>

**Miraflores**

One of Lima's most central and well-connected districts. Convenient for travellers staying in or near the city centre, with easy access to the Malecón coastal road and major arterial routes south toward Ica.

**San Isidro**

Lima's financial district, centrally located and easily reachable from most parts of the city. A practical option if you are arriving from or departing to the northern regions, including Ancash and Piura.

**San Miguel**

Located in western Lima near the coast, San Miguel offers quick access to the Panamericana Norte highway. A good starting point for trips to coastal destinations or for travellers coming from or heading to Lima's port areas.

<Note>
  Pedal's physical office is at **La Libertad 255, La Molina**. For questions about specific location details or directions, contact the team directly before your pickup day.
</Note>
