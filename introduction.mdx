## What is Pedal?

Pedal is a car rental web platform that makes it easy for users in Peru to browse vehicles, choose pickup and drop-off locations, and book the right car for any trip. Whether you need a compact city car or a spacious SUV for a family adventure, Pedal connects you with the right vehicle in minutes.

The platform offers a curated fleet from trusted brands — Honda, Chevrolet, Volvo, Mitsubishi, and Volkswagen — with transparent daily pricing and a straightforward booking experience.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose a location">
    Select the city and pickup point where you want to collect your rental car. Pedal currently serves Miraflores, San Isidro, and San Miguel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set a date and time">
    Pick your pickup date, pickup time, return date, and return time. Pedal offers flexible scheduling to fit your travel plans.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick your vehicle">
    Browse the fleet filtered by brand. Each car shows seating capacity, transmission type, and fuel efficiency so you can choose with confidence.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Collect your car">
    Arrive at the selected location at your scheduled time. The Pedal team will be ready to hand over the keys and make sure everything is in order.
  </Step>
</Steps>

- Lima
- Ica
- Arequipa
- Ancash
- Piura

<Note>
  Pickup and return locations are currently available at **Miraflores**, **San Isidro**, and **San Miguel** in the Lima metropolitan area.
