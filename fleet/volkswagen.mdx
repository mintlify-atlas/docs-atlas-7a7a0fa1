## Best for

**Volkswagen Polo** — The most fuel-efficient vehicle in the entire Pedal fleet at 18 Km/L. The Polo combines this outstanding efficiency with a budget-friendly daily rate of S/. 700, making it the top pick for eco-conscious renters and those covering high mileage in the city or on short intercity routes.

**Volkswagen Passat** — Business-class diesel sedan for professional travellers. The automatic transmission, diesel engine, and 14 Km/L efficiency make the Passat comfortable and economical for longer highway drives and client-facing journeys.

**Volkswagen Tiguan** — 7-seat petrol SUV for families or groups who need space without stepping into the premium price tier. At S/. 1,200/day it sits at the same price point as the Volvo XC40 but offers an extra two seats.

<Note>
  The Volkswagen Polo achieves 18 Km/L — the highest fuel efficiency of any vehicle on Pedal. No other model in the fleet matches this figure.
</Note>
