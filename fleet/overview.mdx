Pedal offers 15 vehicles across five brands — Honda, Chevrolet, Volvo, Mitsubishi, and Volkswagen. The fleet spans compact city cars, business sedans, and 7-seat SUVs, covering a daily rate range from S/. 600 to S/. 1,500. Every vehicle listing on the platform shows a **Rentar ahora** button to start a booking and a **Detalles** button to view full specifications before you commit.
