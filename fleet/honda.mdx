## Best for

**Honda Civic** — Urban commuting and efficient city driving. The automatic transmission makes it easy to navigate stop-and-go traffic, and at S/. 800/day it is the most affordable Honda on the platform.

**Honda Accord** — Longer trips and comfortable highway driving. The manual gearbox gives drivers more control on open roads. Best suited for travellers who prefer a hands-on driving experience.

**Honda CR-V** — Versatile SUV use across city and mixed terrain. The CR-V delivers the best fuel efficiency of the three Honda models at 14 Km/L thanks to its diesel engine, making it the most economical choice for longer journeys.

<Tip>
