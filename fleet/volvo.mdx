## Best for

**Volvo XC40** — Best-value premium SUV. The XC40 offers excellent fuel efficiency for a premium vehicle at 15 Km/L — the highest in the Volvo lineup — while delivering the comfort and build quality the brand is known for. A strong choice for customers who want a premium SUV without the highest price tag.

**Volvo S60** — Premium sedan for comfortable long-distance travel. The manual transmission and petrol engine make it well-suited to highway driving, and the S60 is the most affordable Volvo on the platform at S/. 1,100/day.

**Volvo XC90** — Luxury 7-seater for the ultimate group travel experience. At S/. 1,500/day the XC90 is the most expensive vehicle on Pedal, but it provides maximum passenger comfort, a diesel engine for highway efficiency, and Volvo's full suite of safety features.
