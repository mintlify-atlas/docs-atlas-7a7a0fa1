## Best for

**Chevrolet Spark** — Budget-friendly trips and solo or paired city travel. The Spark is the most fuel-efficient Chevrolet at 16 Km/L and one of the most affordable vehicles on Pedal at S/. 700/day. Its 4-seat compact form is ideal for navigating urban areas.

**Chevrolet Cruze** — Balanced mid-size sedan for city and highway. The automatic transmission and 5-seat capacity make it a comfortable all-rounder for both daily commutes and longer intercity journeys.

**Chevrolet Trailblazer** — Large families and groups requiring 7 seats. The diesel engine and SUV body give it the capability to handle varied road conditions, while the automatic gearbox keeps long drives comfortable.

<Note>
  The Chevrolet Spark has the highest fuel efficiency in the Chevrolet lineup at 16 Km/L, making it the best choice when minimising fuel costs is a priority.
</Note>
