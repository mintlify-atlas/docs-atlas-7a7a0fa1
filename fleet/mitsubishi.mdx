## Best for

**Mitsubishi Mirage** — The most affordable option on Pedal at S/. 600/day and the best overall fuel efficiency in the entire fleet at 17 Km/L. The Mirage is the right choice for solo travellers or small groups who want to minimise both rental and fuel costs on city or short-distance trips.

**Mitsubishi Lancer** — Mid-range automatic sedan for passengers who want a comfortable petrol sedan without the cost of a premium brand. The automatic gearbox makes it easy to drive and suitable for both city and highway use.

**Mitsubishi Pajero** — Rugged 7-seat SUV ideal for off-road routes, multi-passenger trips, or destinations with unpaved roads. The diesel engine supports longer journeys and the automatic transmission makes 7-passenger driving manageable.

<Tip>
  The Mitsubishi Mirage is Pedal's most affordable vehicle overall at S/. 600/day. It also leads the entire fleet in fuel efficiency at 17 Km/L, making it the lowest total-cost option for budget-conscious renters.
