The vehicle catalog lets you explore every car available on Pedal. Vehicles are organized by brand, and a set of brand filter buttons at the top of the catalog lets you switch between them instantly.

## Brand filter

At the top of the catalog page, five brand buttons appear in a row:

- **Honda**
- **Chevrolet**
- **Volvo**
- **Mitsubishi**
- **Volkswagen**

Clicking a brand button shows only the vehicles for that brand and hides all others. Each car in the catalog carries a `data-brand` attribute that the filter reads to show or hide it.

<Note>
  Honda is the default brand displayed when the catalog first loads. All Honda vehicles are visible immediately without clicking any filter button.
</Note>

## Vehicle details

Each vehicle card in the catalog displays the following information:

- **Fuel type** — Petrol or Diesel, shown as a badge on the card.
- **Seats** — The number of passenger seats available.
- **Transmission** — Manual or Automatic.
- **Fuel efficiency** — Kilometres per litre (Km/L).
- **Daily price** — Rental cost in Peruvian soles (S/.) per day.
- **Details button** — Opens the full vehicle description and specifications.
- **Rent now button** — Takes you directly to the booking form for that vehicle. See [Booking a vehicle](/features/booking) for the full reservation flow.

## Booking from the catalog

Once you find a vehicle that fits your needs, click **Rent now** on the vehicle card. This takes you to the booking form where you can select your car type, date, pickup time, and return details. For a full walkthrough of the booking process, see [Booking a vehicle](/features/booking).
