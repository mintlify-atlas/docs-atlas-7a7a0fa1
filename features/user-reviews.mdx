Pedal displays customer testimonials directly on the platform so you can see what other renters have experienced before making a booking. Reviews cover the ease of booking, the range of vehicles available, and the quality of customer service.

## Star rating system

Each review on Pedal is accompanied by a star rating out of five. Stars are displayed visually alongside the reviewer's name and comment. The ratings reflect the overall satisfaction of verified customers who have completed a rental through the platform.

## What customers say

Three recurring themes appear across customer reviews:

- **Easy booking** — Customers consistently highlight how straightforward the reservation process is, from selecting a vehicle to submitting the form.
- **Great vehicle selection** — The variety of brands and models available is frequently mentioned as a standout feature, giving renters the flexibility to choose the right car for any trip.
- **Excellent customer service** — Reviewers praise the responsiveness and helpfulness of the Pedal support team, with multiple customers describing the service as exceptional.
