The booking form on Pedal collects everything needed to prepare your rental in one place. Fill in the fields below in order, then submit to confirm your reservation.

## Booking process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose your car type">
    Select the category of vehicle you want from the **Car type** dropdown. The available categories are Compacto, SedÃ¡n, SUV, and Camioneta. If you are coming from the vehicle catalog, the car type may already be pre-selected based on the vehicle you chose.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your pickup date">
    Use the **Date of use** field to pick the calendar date when you plan to collect the car. The date must be today or a future date.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your pickup time and location">
    Enter the **Pickup time** â€” the hour and minute at which you will arrive to collect the vehicle. Then choose your **Pickup location** from the available options: Miraflores, San Isidro, or San Miguel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your return time and location">
    Enter the **Return time** â€” when you plan to bring the car back. Then select your **Return location**. You can return the vehicle to a different location from where you picked it up.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit the form">
    Review all the details, then click **Submit** to send your reservation request. Pedal's team will confirm your booking and prepare the vehicle at the selected location.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Form fields

<ParamField query="tipo_carro" type="string" required>
  The category of vehicle to rent. Select one of the following options:
  - `Compacto` â€” Compact car, best for city driving.
  - `SedÃ¡n` â€” Standard sedan, suited to longer trips.
  - `SUV` â€” Sport utility vehicle, good for mixed terrain.
  - `Camioneta` â€” Pickup truck, for cargo or off-road use.
</ParamField>

<ParamField query="fecha_uso" type="string" required>
  The date on which the rental period begins. Uses a date input (`YYYY-MM-DD`). Must be a present or future date.
</ParamField>

<ParamField query="hora_recoger" type="string" required>
  The time at which you will collect the vehicle at the pickup location. Uses a time input (`HH:MM`, 24-hour format).
</ParamField>

<ParamField query="lugar_recoger" type="string" required>
  The pickup location. Select one of:
  - `Miraflores`
  - `San Isidro`
  - `San Miguel`
</ParamField>

<ParamField query="hora_devolver" type="string" required>
  The time at which you will return the vehicle to the return location. Uses a time input (`HH:MM`, 24-hour format).
</ParamField>

<ParamField query="lugar_devolver" type="string" required>
  The return location. Select one of:
  - `Miraflores`
  - `San Isidro`
  - `San Miguel`

  This can be the same location as the pickup or a different one.
</ParamField>

<Warning>
  Make sure the return time is set after the pickup time. If your rental spans multiple days, confirm the date and time combination carefully. Submitting a return time earlier than your pickup time may result in an invalid reservation.
</Warning>
