Pedal currently operates three pickup and return locations, all in the Lima metropolitan area. You can collect and return a vehicle at any of these offices — and you are not required to return to the same location you picked up from.

## Pickup and return offices

<Columns cols={3}>
  <Card title="Miraflores" icon="map-pin">
    One of Lima's most visited districts, Miraflores is centrally located and easily accessible from the city center and the coast. Ideal if you're starting your trip from downtown Lima or heading south.
  </Card>
  <Card title="San Isidro" icon="map-pin">
    Lima's financial and business district. San Isidro is well-connected to major highways and is a convenient option if you are travelling for work or staying in the area.
  </Card>
  <Card title="San Miguel" icon="map-pin">
    Located west of Lima near the coast and close to the airport road, San Miguel is a practical choice for travellers arriving or departing via Jorge Chávez International Airport.
  </Card>
</Columns>

<Note>
  You can pick up your vehicle at one location and return it at a different one. For example, collecting at San Miguel and returning at Miraflores is fully supported. Select both locations independently when filling out the [booking form](/features/booking).
</Note>

## Coverage across Peru

Although the physical pickup and return offices are in Lima, Pedal's service covers five departments across Peru:

| Department | Region |
|------------|--------|
| Lima | Coast (Costa) |
| Ica | Coast (Costa) |
| Arequipa | Southern highlands (Sierra Sur) |
| Ancash | Northern highlands (Sierra Norte) |
| Piura | Northern coast (Costa Norte) |

This means you can use a vehicle rented from a Lima office to travel to and across any of these departments.

## Interactive map

The Pedal website includes an interactive Google Map of the Lima area that shows service coverage and helps you identify the most convenient pickup office relative to your starting point. The map is available directly on the platform's main page alongside the location search bar, where you can also search by car type to check availability.
